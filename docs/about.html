About Me

Hello! I'm Sergi Sirvent, a graduate in Interactive Technologies currently studying Video Game Programming at ESAT in Valencia. I'm passionate about applying and expanding my programming skills in a professional environment.

I've always had a strong affinity for mathematics, and through game programming, I’ve been able to combine it with one of my greatest hobbies: video games.

I’m an organized, proactive, and respectful person who thrives in team environments. I’ve actively participated in projects using agile methodologies like SCRUM and feel comfortable working within that framework.

I earned my degree in Interactive Technologies at the Polytechnic University of Valencia (UPV) from 2019 to 2023. In my final year, I joined an exchange program at ENTI-UB in Barcelona. Currently, I’m continuing my studies at ESAT Valencia.

I also hold a Cambridge B2 English certificate and I’m currently learning Japanese.

You can find several of my published projects on my Itch.io profile — I’m always exploring new game ideas and creative projects.
